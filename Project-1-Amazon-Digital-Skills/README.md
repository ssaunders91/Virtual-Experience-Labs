# 🛡️ Project 1: Amazon Digital Skills – Cybersecurity

### 👋 Overview
I completed this programme with **Amazon Future Engineer** and **Springpod** to get a proper look at how a global tech giant like Amazon stays secure. It wasn't just about the tech; it was about understanding how security fits into a massive business that handles millions of customers' data every single day.

---

## 🧠 What I Learnt

### 1. The Modern Threat Landscape
* Looked into how hackers actually work today—moving beyond simple viruses to more complex **Phishing** and **Social Engineering** attacks.
* Explored the **"Defence in Depth"** approach, which is basically about having multiple layers of security so if one fails, the others are there to catch the threat.

### 2. Cloud Security (The AWS Way)
* Got to grips with **Amazon Web Services (AWS)** and how they protect the "Cloud."
* Learnt about the **Shared Responsibility Model**. This was a big one: Amazon secures the physical servers, but the users are responsible for securing the data they put *on* those servers.

### 3. Identity & Privacy
* Studied **IAM (Identity and Access Management)**—essentially making sure only the right people have the "keys" to the right digital doors.
* Learnt about the legal side of things, like **GDPR**, and why protecting user privacy isn't just a "nice to have," it's the law.

### 4. Real-World Security Careers
* Heard from actual Amazon Security Engineers and SOC Analysts about what their day-to-day life is like. It gave me a much clearer picture of the different paths I can take in the industry.

---

## 🛠️ Skills & Concepts
* **Concepts:** Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), Encryption, Zero Trust.
* **Frameworks:** Understanding the basics of NIST and ISO standards.
* **Soft Skills:** Risk Assessment, Digital Ethics, and Problem Solving.
